graphics

This section brings together different graphic and web projects created over the years — logos, websites, business cards, printed materials and visual pieces made for different people, places and needs. The visual language changes from project to project, because every person, place and story brings its own rhythm. Some works are quiet and simple, others more decorative or practical — each one belongs to a different moment and a different conversation.

Graphic work has been with me for a long time: first as a hobby, then slowly as part of my professional life. I learned InDesign during my studies, used GIMP for personal experiments, worked with CorelDRAW in my first design-related jobs, and later moved into Illustrator and the Adobe workflow.

audio & video

My first film projects were made around 25 years ago on a borrowed VHS camera — school documentaries about difficult post-war memory, national identity and the fragile relationships shaped by history. Later came subtitle frames in VirtualDub, school events, sailing videos and small visual experiments. Today, through AI media and content creation, this old interest has returned in a new form: bringing hidden texts, old ideas and unfinished moods back to life.

The process usually starts with my own lyrics, mood and sound direction. I shape the rhythm of the text, refine the phrasing, build the prompt, generate and select the track, then continue working with stems, voice tools, mixing and mastering.

paint

Painting is a quiet place for me.

A way to slow down emotions from the day — without explaining them too much, without making them useful, without making them perfect. Imperfect honesty.
